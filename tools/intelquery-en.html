About Google Dorks & OSINT

What are Google Dorks?

Google Dorks are advanced search operators that allow you to find specific information indexed by Google. They are essential tools for security researchers, pentesters, and OSINT (Open Source Intelligence) professionals.

Use Cases

  • Identify publicly exposed sensitive documents
  • Discover accessible directories and configuration files
  • Map the attack surface during reconnaissance
  • Check for credential and API key leaks
  • Find administrative panels and login pages
